Business Process Outsourcing

CFOs across all industries share a common goal: to create a world-class finance organization. Today, that means more than an efficient and effective finance organization. To achieve optimum performance in a challenging global marketplace, companies are looking to their finance function for insights that will drive business improvement.

Our 6 Steps Approach to achieve CFO’s agenda on Outsourced Finance & Accounting:
1. Identify the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) - of the Finance and Accounts Department – This helps align all processes being outsourced to align to the KPIs.
2. Identify the critical paths to achieve the KPIs – Of all the tasks performed, some will be on the critical path to achieve the KPIs and when these are identified, it increases the control on performance to achieve the KPIs.
3. Assemble a global task allocation calendar to optimize cost effective operation – In a global outsourcing scenario, competencies from different geographies are involved in the Finance and Accounts Department.
4. Tighten grip on departmental budgets and performance against budgets through timely reporting and tracking - Managing costs is such a high priority and there is perhaps no better approach to address this issue for a CFO than have an efficient budgetary control.
5. Establish an efficient knowledge management though good documentation practice - A good knowledge management practice ensures that business continuity is unaffected by people leaving the processes. It also helps in troubleshooting and analysis for improving the current processes.
6. Competent IT support that can provide Technology transformation
