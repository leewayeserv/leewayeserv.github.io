Travel BPO services :- 

Travel BPO Services is fortified with the business experiences and relationships that the outsourcing companies carry on with international travel companies or airlines.

According to a recent report, a majority of companies use outsourcing as a vehicle of transformation. The companies who outsource, experience a lot of pressure to provide global integration. Adapting to business processes through a BPO model involves a lot of saving on the labor cost through a low cost location for the off shoring process. The outsourcing initiative is more strategic now. Apart from optimization of technology, the other important features are labor arbitrage, important economies of scale outsourcing, and an improvement process that is continuous
The CFOs and CEOs are looking to drive these assets beyond the issues of cost and to transform the overall performance, shareholders’ value, and competitiveness. For these companies, the BPO is a wonderful business life and continues to make its mark as the prime factor in generating efficiency and cutting costs.

There are many BPO companies that carry expertise in the travel domain taking the help of professionals in the travel industry. These include various streams like Travel Agency operations, travel agency fares, GDSs and the airline processes. This is further strengthened by the business experience shared by international customers.

 Leeway brings out the technology expertise to build the core experts in areas of revenue integrity, interlining, and revenue management, check in, ticketing, baggage handling, frequent fliers problem solving.

When the core team is up and going, Leeway can provide solutions to the travel companies and the airlines to enhance their productivity and also the Return on Investment from the IT investments. This is done by the process of consulting, creating an architectural roadmap, strategy planning of ecommerce, web integration, interfacing applications, outsourcing solutions for processes that are non core like testing, support, maintenance and other enhancements.

The outsourcing companies which are the leaders in the market have their own partners and technology professionals in the realm of travel technology. They also have multi airlines experiences and they are the leaders in their respective areas. So with these capabilities, the outsourcing firms have helped large travel agencies and airlines put forward their roadmaps and deliver successfully in the areas of Agency Back Office automation, revenue management, departure control, reservations and e-ticketing.
